About me

Mechanical Project Engineer with over 8 years of experience in the design, coordination, and validation of complex mechanical systems for scientific research infrastructures (IFMIF- DONES and ALBA–CELLS). Proven background in vacuum systems, beamline components, and accelerator engineering. Experienced in synchrotron instrumentation, CAD/FEM/CFD design, vacuum and cryogenic systems, and beamline mechanical integration, from conceptual to detailed design. Llorenç Macià is an Assistant Professor at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), Barcelona. He holds a Ph.D. in Fluid Mechanics and Aeronautics (2026), and a M.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineer (2016). His current interests include Vacuum Technologies, Particle Accelerators, and Nuclear Energy.
