About me

Llorenç Macià is an Assistant Professor at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), Barcelona. He holds a M.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineering (2016) from UPC, an D.Sc. degree in Industrial Technologies (2014). His current interests include Vacuum Technologies, Supersonic Nozzle, and OpenFOAM.
